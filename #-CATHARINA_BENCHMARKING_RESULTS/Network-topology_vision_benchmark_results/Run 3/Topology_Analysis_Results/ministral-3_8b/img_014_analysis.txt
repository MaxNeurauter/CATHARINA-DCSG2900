- **No Logical Segmentation:**
  - No indication of VLANs or subnets to segment traffic (e.g., separating VoIP, management, or guest networks). This increases the risk of lateral movement in case of a breach.

   - **Implement strict access controls** (e.g., ACLs, micro-segmentation) to limit traffic between VLANs.
   - **Enable logging and monitoring** on all security devices to detect anomalies and breaches in real time.
