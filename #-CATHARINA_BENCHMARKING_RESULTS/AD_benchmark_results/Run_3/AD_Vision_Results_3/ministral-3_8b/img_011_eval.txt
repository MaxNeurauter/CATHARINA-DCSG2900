### **3. Attack Path (If Exploitable)**
#### **Potential Attack Vector: Privilege Escalation via GPO Tampering**
- **Technical Impact:**
  - If an attacker gains access to a compromised account (e.g., `samwelltarly` or `vagrant`), they could **modify the GPO** to:

- **Why This Matters:**
  - GPOs are **highly privileged** in Active Directory. Misconfigured GPO permissions can lead to **lateral movement** and **domain-wide compromise**.
  - The presence of **non-admin users with GPO edit rights** suggests a lack of **least privilege enforcement**.
