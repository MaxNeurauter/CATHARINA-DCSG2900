### **3. Attack Path**
#### **Primary Attack Vector: Golden Ticket / Silver Ticket Attack**
- **Technical Impact:**
  - An attacker who gains the **NTLM hash** (or cracks it) of this user can forge **Kerberos tickets** for **any user/service** in the domain.
  - If the attacker targets the **`krbtgt` account**, they can create **Golden Tickets** (persistent domain dominance).
  - If targeting a **specific service account**, they can create **Silver Tickets** (limited but powerful lateral movement).
  - This allows **full domain takeover**, **privilege escalation**, and **unauthorized access** to all resources.

#### **Secondary Attack Vector: Kerberoasting**
- While Kerberoasting typically targets **service accounts**, an attacker could still **brute-force the hash** of this delegated user to gain a foothold.
