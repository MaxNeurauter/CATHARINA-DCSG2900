*   **Technical Impact (Kerberoasting):** The "Account is sensitive" flag, when checked, instructs Kerberos to encrypt the Kerberos Ticket Granting Ticket (TGT) using a stronger encryption type (typically AES) and restrict its use only to services on the same domain controller. If an attacker can obtain a TGT (e.g., through Kerberoasting of a service account), they can then use it to request service tickets for other services without knowing the service account password.  The weaker encryption also makes cracking the TGT easier for an attacker.
