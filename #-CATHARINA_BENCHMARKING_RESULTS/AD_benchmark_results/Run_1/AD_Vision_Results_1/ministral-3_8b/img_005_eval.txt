- **Impact:**

  - If combined with **pass-the-hash** or **pass-the-ticket** techniques, a compromised account could lead to **lateral movement** and **privilege escalation**.
  - **Denial-of-Service (DoS) risk** if attackers repeatedly lock out legitimate users (e.g., via automated scripts).

- **Why This Works:**
  - The **5-minute lockout duration** allows attackers to **rotate attempts** before the account is locked.
  - The **5-minute reset window** means the lockout counter resets quickly, enabling **persistent brute-force attempts**.
  - If the domain uses **Kerberos**, an attacker could perform **AS-REP Roasting** (if **Account is sensitive and cannot be reset** is enabled) or **Golden Ticket attacks** if they obtain hashes.
