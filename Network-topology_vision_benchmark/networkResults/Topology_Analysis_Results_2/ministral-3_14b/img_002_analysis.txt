#### **4. Weaknesses & Risks (Cons)**

❌ **Lack of Security Boundaries**
   - **No firewall, ACLs, or DMZ** → Vulnerable to **lateral movement attacks**.
   - **No segmentation** → A compromised device could affect the entire subnet.

❌ **No Clear Hierarchy (Violates Cisco 3-Tier Model)**
   - Missing:
     - **Core Layer** (high-speed routing between subnets).
     - **Distribution Layer** (policy enforcement, routing).
