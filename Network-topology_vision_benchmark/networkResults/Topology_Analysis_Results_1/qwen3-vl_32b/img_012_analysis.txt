- **Security & Performance Risks**:  
  - **No segmentation**: All PCs reside in the same broadcast domain. A compromised PC (e.g., PC0) can access all other devices (e.g., PC7, PC8), violating the principle of least privilege.  
  - **Broadcast traffic explosion**: As the network grows, broadcast domains (e.g., ARP requests) will degrade performance.
